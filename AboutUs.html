OUR HISTORY

FArbucks intended to be a distinct kind of company from the start. One that not only honored coffee and its long history, but also fostered a sense of community. FArbucks, which was founded in December 2021 by Adam Schindler and Anwar Wibowo, is currently a coffee shop with locations all over the world and is one of the world's fastest-growing coffee chains. It sells a variety of coffee and hosts thought-provoking and entertaining events in order to strengthen and enhance the coffee community. FArbucks appeared for the first time in Seattle and Jakarta, as a small coffee shop serving solely Indonesian coffee, but it soon became increasingly popular and demanding, requiring us to grow and expand our business. Over the years, Farbucks grew to welcome hundreds of customers each week and has become a part of the history of hundreds of communities throughout the world. From the most premium, exotic, and one-of-a-kind coffees to the most basic style of coffee available, we now provide it all. Each of these coffees, which come from various countries, has a unique flavor and a captivating story to offer.

INNOVATIONS

We are passionate about finding new ways to develop and make things better. Innovations within the coffee market will enable us to explore new consumption occasions and enhance convenience and memorable caffeinated experiences.

Nitro Cold Brew

One of our most popular coffee innovations of all time is the nitro cold brew. Nitro cold brew includes mixing coffees, cold brew coffees and nitrogen coffees, together to give the beverage a large, frothy crema-style head.

Mushroom Lattes

FArbucks wellness groups have found common ground in an unlikely ingredient. Mushroom Coffee has a lesser amount of caffeine and is fortified with Lion’s Mane & Chaga mushrooms to help you gain the same benefits as regular coffees.
