OUR WEEKLY EVENTS

Stay informed and know what is happening with previews covering the latest and upcoming weekly events throughout our global community. FArbucks provides different kinds of events that engage the specialty coffee community and promote coffee excellence. We support the professional specialty coffee community by providing a platform for a thriving coffee community through the presence of events. They enable people to get together, talk, share knowledge, and collaborate. They serve as a starting point, opportunities, and inspiration for creativity and further development.

THIS WEEK’S EVENTS

Triestespresso Expo
27 June 2022
03:00 PM PT
Jl. Metro Pondok Indah, South Jakarta, Indonesia

Triestespresso Expo is a networking event for experts in the coffee industry from across the world. It is a highly recommended and a must-attend event for coffee professionals, offering B2B meetings, a full calendar of side activities focusing on education and innovation, and the complete coffee chain on display, from bean to cup.

Roasting Masterclass
2 July 2022
10:00 AM PT
Jl. Pangeran Antasari, South Jakarta, Indonesia

This event will offer and cover topics such as discussion of a new Roast Defect Kit, tasting of delicious coffees to demonstrate ideal roasting practices and results, how grind styles affect brewing and flavour, how water affects the taste of coffee, and many more. This event will also have a Q&A session as well as coffee sampling and brewing demonstrations.
